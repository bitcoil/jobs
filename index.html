Bitcoin is our passion

In 2011, Meni Rosenfeld founded Bitcoil, the first Israeli Bitcoin exchange. Together with Ron Gross, he founded the Israeli Bitcoin Community, a multi-faceted organization of Bitcoin enthusiasts numbering over a thousand people today.

In January 2013, Ron joined Meni as a partner in Bitcoil, and together they are building the best Israeli exchange, and more.

Now, we are looking to enlarge our core team - this is where you come in.

We are looking for a Chief Operations Officer, a co-founder and partner in Bitcoil. As a COO, you will take ownership of all operational aspects of building and running the exchange, and our other ongoing projects. You will be a key partner in the company, help us make stuff happen and help Israeli customers buy and sell our beloved new currency.

Job Requirements:

  1. Relevant Experience. If you were a COO or an officer of a company, that's terrific. Even if you didn't hold such a title, but have the experience to back it up, that's good too.
  2. Passion for Bitcoin and new forms of currency. We think Bitcoin is the best thing since sliced bread, and so should you.
  3. Desire to make stuff happen and take ownership. We need someone who will solve all of our operational problems and will just Get Shit Done.
  4. Bonus - experience with the Israeli and international banking system, payment processing, and regulatory systems.
